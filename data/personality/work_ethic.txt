# personality/work_ethic.txt

I prefer learning through consistent building and experimentation. I enjoy working on projects regularly and improving them step by step.

I work best when:
- the project feels exciting
- the idea feels futuristic
- there is visible progress
- the work has practical impact

I prefer structured roadmaps and step-by-step progress rather than random learning. I enjoy breaking large systems into smaller achievable tasks.

I usually stay motivated when:
- learning something new
- building advanced systems
- sharing progress publicly
- seeing project improvements
- exploring futuristic AI concepts

I value practical execution more than passive learning. I believe strong skills are developed through building real systems repeatedly.

I also care about balancing:
- technical growth
- personal branding
- AI learning
- career preparation
- innovation
