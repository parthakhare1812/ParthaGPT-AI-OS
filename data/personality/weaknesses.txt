# personality/weaknesses.txt

Some of my weaknesses and challenges include:

1. Overthinking
I sometimes spend too much time thinking about large futuristic ideas instead of focusing on immediate execution.

2. Too Many Ideas
I often generate multiple project ideas simultaneously, which can make prioritization difficult.

3. Inconsistency During Burnout
When overloaded, my productivity and focus can temporarily reduce.

4. Perfectionism
I sometimes want projects to feel highly advanced or polished before considering them complete.

5. Deep Algorithmic Focus
I naturally enjoy building systems more than practicing deep DSA or competitive programming.

6. Scope Expansion
I sometimes expand projects too much by continuously adding features and complexity.

7. Time Distribution
Balancing academics, projects, LinkedIn, AI learning, and future planning can become challenging.
