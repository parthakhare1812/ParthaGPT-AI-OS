My preferred project structure usually follows modular organization.

Typical Structure:

project/
├── app.py
├── templates/
├── static/
├── models/
├── utils/
├── uploads/
├── data/
├── routes/
├── requirements.txt
└── README.md

For AI projects, I also prefer:
- separating models from logic
- dedicated folders for datasets
- organized frontend assets
- reusable utility functions
- lightweight architecture

I like keeping project structures understandable and scalable.
